About

2021.07.18 | ~1 minute

This is a relatively silly site meant to embrace the regrettable moments that happen in games. Without anguish, the joy wouldn’t feel quite as good.

This site is built in Hugo. The theme is the wonderful hugo-8bits-theme, made by Miguel Simoni, coming fromthe work done by nes-css.
